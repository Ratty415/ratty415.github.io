    Study in Europe

    Study in Europe has been offering high quality consultation and support services to prospective international students in Europe since 2014. Excellent client relationship is very important in our service delivery. That differentiates us from others and we look forward to assisting you in finding the right university.

    High Quality Education

    The best education you can get in Europe is relatively free compared to other countries around the world. The demand for higher education in Europe has skyrocketed due to subsidised education, labour market demands and stable economy and has resulted to the high conducive learning environment in Europe.

    Work and Study

    Nearly all the countries in Europe allow foreign students to work while they study. This is a great opportunity for students to develop themselves, acquire job experience and earn income to support living and study expenses.

    ERASMUS Exchange Programmes

    The ERASMUS Exchange Programme is a unique programme that offers Euopean students the opportunity to spend up to two semesters or complete an entire degree abroad. This offers students the chances to interact with people of other cultures and learn foreign languages.

    Services

    Services

    Academic Support

    Personalised consultation in career choice and choice of appropriate university to maximise your potentials and goals.

    Admission Application

    We handle your applications for admissions from the very beginning until you recieve your admission letter.

    Visa Processing Support

    After admission, we give guidance on the necessary visa procedure and documentation that leads to getting a student/work visa and resident permit.

    Arrival and Pick-up Services

    We help to arrange for your arrival in your new country and where necessary also pick you up from the airport to your residence.

    Transfer to Another University

    For regular students who wish to transfer to a European university, we support you through your application and transfer of your studies to another university.

    Resident Permit Support

    We support you in application, renewal and change of purpose of resident permit.
